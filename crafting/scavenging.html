Scavenging

General rules

All checks made for scavenging are survival checks. You can gather food, water, wood for fireplace or just look for useful things in the area.

  • If you move at half the speed you can make one scavenging check per 4 hours of walk.
  • If you are stationary you can spend 1 hour scavenging for resources.
  • Check for limited resource can only be repeated in same area after 1 day.
  • You can scavange for only one category of resources at once
  • Only 2 characters can look for same resource at once, each of them making their own check
